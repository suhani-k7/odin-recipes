Description

These sweet potato fries make a great side dish and are sure to please your friends and family. Season as you please!

Ingredients:

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Oil
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Peeling: Peel the sweet potatoes and cut into wedges.
  2. Toss: Toss the wedges in oil, then season them.
  3. Bake: Bake, turning occasionally, until the fries are tender and golden brown.
